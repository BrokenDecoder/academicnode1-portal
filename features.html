Core Capabilities

Nine STEM-focused modules designed for students, researchers, and curious minds.

01 Deep STEM Research Generator

Create detailed reports on any STEM topic including overview, core concepts, subfields, applications, challenges, future scope, and resources.

  • Structured multi-section reports
  • Cited resources and reading lists
  • Beginner to advanced depth control

02 STEM News Feed

Latest STEM news, breakthroughs, technology updates, space discoveries, AI developments, engineering innovations, climate updates, and academic highlights.

  • Filters: Science, Tech, Engineering, Math, AI, Space, Health, Environment, Robotics, Education
  • Summary format: title, date, source, field, importance, impact
  • Verified facts clearly separated from predictions

03 Research Paper Analyzer

Analyze papers via title, abstract, PDF, DOI, or link. Extract everything that matters.

  • Problem statement & methodology
  • Results, limitations, equations
  • Datasets and future work suggestions

04 Literature Review Generator

Summarize key themes, compare multiple studies, identify research gaps, and suggest future directions.

05 Career & Skill Advisor

Personalized STEM career paths, skills to learn, tools to master, certifications, portfolio projects, and learning roadmaps.

06 Learning Roadmap Builder

Beginner-to-advanced study plans with timelines, topics, milestone projects, and curated resources.

07 Project & Experiment Generator

STEM project ideas tailored to your field, difficulty, budget, duration, and available tools.

08 Problem Solver

Step-by-step solutions for math, physics, engineering, coding, statistics, and science problems.

09 Visualization & Simulation Helper

Generate diagrams, graphs, flowcharts, model explanations, experiment designs, and system architectures.
